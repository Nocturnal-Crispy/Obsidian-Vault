#### Fed holds at 3.50–3.75% but turns hawkish — inflation revised up sharply
`Heat: High` · `Momentum: NEW` · `Tier 1 — Confirmed` · `Category: Macro`

- **What:** At its June 17 meeting (first under new Chair Kevin Warsh) the Fed held the funds rate at 3.50–3.75% for a fourth straight meeting.

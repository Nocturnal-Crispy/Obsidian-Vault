#### 1099 reporting thresholds in effect for 2026 (OBBBA)
`Heat: Medium` · `Momentum: SUSTAINED` · `Tier 1 — Confirmed` · `Category: Policy`

- **What:** Under the One Big Beautiful Bill Act, 1099-MISC/1099-NEC reporting minimums rose from $600 to **$2,000** for payments made in 2026 (inflation-indexed from 2027). 1099-K reverts to the pre-2022 **$20,000 / 200-transaction** threshold.

# Gaming Intelligence — Tuesday, March 31, 2026

## 1. Launch Day: Super Meat Boy 3D and Legacy of Kain: Ascendance
**Heat: High | Momentum: NEW | Source: Tier 1 (Team Meat, Crystal Dynamics, Xbox Wire, PlayStation Blog)**

Two notable launches today. Super Meat Boy 3D arrives on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch 2, and PC — day one on Game Pass. The fully 3D reimagining features boss encounters, expanded secret levels, and a Dark World variant. Early reception describes it as "infuriating" in the best sense. Legacy of Kain: Ascendance launches as a 2D action platformer prequel to Soul Reaver on all major platforms. Players control Kain, Raziel (human and vampiric forms), and new character Elaleth. This is the franchise's first new entry in over two decades, paired with the Defiance Remastered release.

## 2. ARC Raiders Flashpoint Update — Sustained Momentum
**Heat: High | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 1 (Embark Studios), Tier 3 (SteamDB)**

Embark Studios released the Flashpoint update for ARC Raiders, adding a new ARC, weapons, and more. The cooperative sci-fi shooter has maintained extraordinary momentum in early access — all-time peak of 481,966 concurrent players, with sustained daily peaks of 250K–300K. An email blast to players today signals ongoing content cadence. This is one of the strongest early access runs in recent memory.

## 3. South of Midnight Expands to PS5 and Switch 2
**Heat: Medium | Momentum: NEW | Source: Tier 1 (Xbox Wire, Games Asylum)**

Compulsion Games' action-adventure South of Midnight arrives on PS5 and Switch 2 this week, expanding from its Xbox/PC exclusive launch. The Southern Gothic folklore game has drawn strong critical reception. This is part of Microsoft's broader multiplatform strategy for first-party titles.

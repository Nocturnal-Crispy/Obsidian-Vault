### NSF SBIR/STTR Phase I — Developing Deep Technologies (NSF 26-510 / 26-511)
**Heat: Medium | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Type: Grant (non-repayable)**

NSF's FY2026 Phase I solicitations are open: **up to $305,000** for 6–18 months. Next full-proposal deadline **July 27, 2026 (33 days away)**, with later windows Nov 4, 2026 and Mar 4, 2027. **Process catch:** NSF requires an *invited* Project Pitch before a full proposal. To realistically make July 27, the Project Pitch needs to go in immediately — this window is closing fast.

**Eligibility fit (Dev):** Strong if incorporated as a US for-profit small business (majority US-owned). Requires deep-tech / R&D framing — not routine software consulting.

Apply: [NSF SBIR Solicitations](https://seedfund.nsf.gov/solicitations/) · [NSF 26-510](https://www.nsf.gov/funding/opportunities/small-business-innovation-research-small-business-technology/nsf26-510/solicitation)

### Apple Siri AI Overhaul Targeting iOS 26.4 — Architecture Rebuilt on Gemini
**Heat: High**
**Trend: New Trend**

Apple is rolling out its completely rebuilt Siri with iOS 26.4 this month, replacing the legacy architecture with an LLM-based system powered by a partnership with Google's Gemini model. The upgrade promises on-screen awareness, cross-app actions, and personalized context. However, engineering challenges — particularly a hybrid architecture blending old and new systems that failed in ~33% of test cases — have caused delays, and some features may slip to iOS 27 in September.

**Who is affected:** Apple's 1B+ iPhone users, Google (Gemini partnership revenue), competing voice assistants, app developers integrating Siri.
**What to watch:** iOS 26.4 release date confirmation; which features ship vs. slip to iOS 27; real-world performance vs. demos; developer API access for agentic capabilities.

**Heat: High**
**Trend: Escalating Trend**

The GlassWorm campaign has evolved its tactics, now abusing Open VSX extension pack dependencies to create transitive delivery vehicles — initially clean-looking extensions that become malicious through their dependency chains.

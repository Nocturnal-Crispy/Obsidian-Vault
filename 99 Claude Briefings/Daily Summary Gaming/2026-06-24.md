# Gaming Intelligence — 2026-06-24

## EMPULSE Hits Steam Early Access Today
**Heat: Medium | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: NEW**

EMPULSE, a movement-heavy arena shooter, entered Steam Early Access today (June 24). It blends Splitgate-style fast-paced gunplay with contestable mechs; a movement-focused demo dropped just ahead of launch. It headlines a busy release day that also includes Happy's Humble BURGATORY (sim/horror cooking), Corelith (action roguelike), Hexara (puzzle), and YGGDRA: SURVIVOR (roguelike party RPG).

**Why it matters:** Arena shooters with novel movement mechanics are a recurring source of surprise Steam breakouts. Worth watching concurrent-player traction over the first week.

Sources: [PC Gamer](https://www.pcgamer.com/games/pc-game-release-dates-june-2026/), [Steam Upcoming](https://store.steampowered.com/explore/upcoming/)

## Star Fox (Switch 2) Launches Tomorrow — June 25
**Heat: High | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: RISING | Countdown: 1 day**

Velan Studios' Star Fox reimagining arrives exclusively on Switch 2 tomorrow (June 25). A reworking of Star Fox 64 with full voice acting, orchestral score, first- and third-person modes, and a 4v4 online Battle Mode. Free demo has been live since June 19. $49.99 digital / $59.99 physical.

**Why it matters:** First major Star Fox in years and a key Switch 2 system-seller heading into summer. Strong demo reception could lift Switch 2 hardware momentum.

Sources: [Nintendo](https://www.nintendo.com/us/store/products/star-fox-switch-2/), [Nintendo Life](https://www.nintendolife.com/news/2026/05/star-fox-is-finally-getting-another-game-out-june-2026-exclusively-on-switch-2)

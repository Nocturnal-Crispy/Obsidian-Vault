### Anthropic Sues Pentagon Over Supply Chain Blacklisting
Anthropic filed a lawsuit on March 9 against the Trump administration after being designated a "supply chain risk" — a label typically reserved for entities tied to foreign adversaries. The dispute stems from Anthropic's insistence on two contractual red lines: no autonomous weapons and no mass surveillance of U.S. citizens. The Pentagon issued the designation after contract negotiations broke down. Anthropic argues the action is unconstitutional, violating free speech and due process. The designation forces defense contractors to certify they don't use Claude in Pentagon work, potentially jeopardizing hundreds of millions in revenue.

**What changed:** A leading AI company is now in open legal conflict with the U.S. military over AI ethics guardrails.
**Who is affected:** Anthropic, defense contractors using Claude, the broader AI industry's relationship with military procurement, competing AI firms (OpenAI, Google) who may benefit.
**What to watch:** Court proceedings; whether other AI companies face similar pressure to loosen safety restrictions; impact on Anthropic's $19B annualized revenue trajectory.

### GlassWorm Supply Chain Attack Escalates — 72 Open VSX Extensions, 151 GitHub Repos
The GlassWorm campaign has expanded significantly since January, now affecting at least 72 malicious Open VSX extensions mimicking popular developer tools (linters, formatters, AI coding assistants) and 151 GitHub repositories compromised between March 3–9. The malware uses invisible Unicode characters and a novel C2 mechanism leveraging Solana blockchain transactions with Google Calendar as fallback. It installs a full RAT and steals credentials.

**What changed:** Major escalation in scope — from VS Code marketplace to Open VSX and GitHub. The blockchain-based C2 is a sophisticated evasion technique.
**Who is affected:** All developers using VS Code extensions and Open VSX; open source supply chain integrity.
**What to watch:** Whether the campaign has downstream effects on production systems; extension marketplace security reforms.
**Trend:** Escalating Trend

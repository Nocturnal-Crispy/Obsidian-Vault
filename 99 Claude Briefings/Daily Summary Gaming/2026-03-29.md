# Gaming Intelligence — Sunday, March 29, 2026

## Story 1: Sony PlayStation Dynamic Pricing — Spring Sale Backlash Continues
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** RISING | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)
**Categories:** Gaming, Market Signals

Sony's individualized pricing system continues across the 2026 Spring Sale (through April 22) with 189 games subject to algorithmic pricing across North America and Europe. Reports of identical games showing different discounts to different players continue to surface. Sony has made no public acknowledgment of the system's existence or methodology.

The story has now persisted for multiple days without Sony issuing a response — a pattern that typically precedes either regulatory attention or a quiet rollback. European Digital Markets Act enforcement bodies have not yet commented publicly.

**Why it matters:** This is now a sustained controversy entering its second week. Sony's silence is itself a signal — the company appears to be testing whether the backlash will dissipate without intervention. If it doesn't, regulatory action in the EU is the most likely forcing function. The precedent this sets for the entire digital storefront ecosystem (Xbox, Steam, Epic) makes this the most important pricing story in gaming since loot box legislation.

## Story 2: Super Meat Boy 3D and Legacy of Kain: Ascendance Launch Tomorrow
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** NEW | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)

Two significant launches arrive March 31:

**Super Meat Boy 3D** (PS5, Xbox Series, Switch 2, PC) — Day one on Xbox Game Pass. The beloved 2D platformer franchise transitions to 3D with boss encounters, expanded secret levels, and Dark World variants. This is the franchise's biggest moment since the original 2010 release.

**Why it matters:** Super Meat Boy 3D on Game Pass day one continues Microsoft's strategy of securing indie launches for the subscription service. Legacy of Kain's revival tests whether dormant IP can be successfully resurrected — a trend increasingly important as publishers mine their back catalogs.

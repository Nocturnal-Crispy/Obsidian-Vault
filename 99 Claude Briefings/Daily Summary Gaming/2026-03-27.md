## Story 2: Life is Strange: Reunion — Reviews Landing, Strong Steam Reception
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** SUSTAINED | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)

Life is Strange: Reunion launched yesterday and early reception is strong. Steam user reviews are "Very Positive" (4.83/5 on PlayStation Store). Critics who managed same-day coverage gave scores in the 8-8.5/10 range, calling it one of the franchise's strongest entries and a worthy finale for Max and Chloe's story.

The unusual no-pre-release-review approach meant most outlets received codes at or just before launch, so the full critical picture is still developing. Review scores are expected to continue rolling in through the weekend.

**Why it matters:** The strong user reception is the key signal. Life is Strange: Reunion appears to have successfully delivered what fans wanted after the mixed reception of Double Exposure. The franchise's future — including a confirmed TV series adaptation — looks more secure. Full Metacritic aggregate will be the definitive measure once reviews settle next week.

## Story 4: Bongo Cat — Still Holding at 170K+, Defying Pattern
**Heat:** Medium | **Momentum:** SUSTAINED | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)

Bongo Cat continues to maintain 24-hour peaks in the 170-180K range (all-time peak 194K) on Steam. The free-to-play meme rhythm game has now sustained above 100K concurrent for over a week. It survived the mega-launch day competition from Life is Strange: Reunion, Mario Wonder Switch 2, and the Xbox Partner Preview without losing significant ground.

**Why it matters:** Bongo Cat has now officially broken the meme-game crash pattern established by Banana Game and Egg in 2024-2025. Its sustained numbers through a major competing launch window suggest genuine genre engagement rather than pure novelty. Worth watching whether it stabilizes at a new baseline or begins a gradual decline through April.

# Gaming Intelligence — Thursday, May 28, 2026

---

## 1. 007 First Light Launches to Strong Reviews
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** NEW | **Source:** Tier 1 (Metacritic, official reviews)

IO Interactive's James Bond game launched May 27 on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC, and Nintendo Switch 2. Early reception is positive — reviewers praise the adaptation of Hitman's stealth formula to the Bond franchise, calling it the best Bond game ever made.

Key features include social stealth with conversation manipulation, disguises, gadgets, and environmental problem-solving. Criticism focuses on linear mission segments and vehicle gameplay.

Nvidia released a day-one Game Ready driver specifically for the title, indicating significant PC market expectations.

**Strategic Impact:** This validates IO Interactive's multi-year bet on the Bond license. Strong sales would greenlight a franchise. The Switch 2 port also demonstrates the new console's capability for AAA third-party titles.

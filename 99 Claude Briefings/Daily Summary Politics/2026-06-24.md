# Politics Intelligence — 2026-06-24

## ROAD to Housing Act Heads to Trump's Desk — Signing Expected Today
**Heat: Critical | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: RISING (escalation → resolution)**

The 21st Century ROAD to Housing Act — passed by the Senate 85-5 (June 22) and House 358-32 (June 23) — went to President Trump, who is supportive. Reporting indicated a signing ceremony was likely at the Capitol on Wednesday (June 24), tied to his scheduled Senate GOP lunch. The law caps institutional investors at 350 single-family homes, removes the manufactured-home permanent-chassis requirement, streamlines environmental reviews, and adds grant funding and homebuilding pilot programs.

**Why it matters:** This converts yesterday's landmark passage into enacted law — the most significant federal housing intervention in decades, and a rare bipartisan win months ahead of the midterms. The manufactured-housing reform (cutting $5K–$10K per unit) and corporate-investor cap directly target affordability and the post-2020 institutional buyup.

## Supreme Court Enters Final Stretch — 22 Cases Left, Including Birthright Citizenship
**Heat: High | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: RISING**

The Supreme Court is racing to finish its term by end of June / early July, with ~22 of 58 argued cases still undecided. Pending blockbusters include the executive order to end birthright citizenship, the president's at-will firing power over independent-agency heads, TPS terminations (Haiti/Syria test cases), mail-ballot rules, and a Second Amendment case. Already decided this term: a ruling gutting much of the Voting Rights Act (prompting Southern redistricting) and one striking down Trump's tariff program as unauthorized by Congress.

**Why it matters:** The remaining rulings could reshape executive power, immigration, and election administration ahead of the 2026 midterms. Expect a dense cluster of high-impact decisions over the next 1–2 weeks.

Sources: [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/09/nx-s1-5847967/supreme-court-major-cases-left-2026), [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/06/03/politics/supreme-court-explosive-final-month-birthright-cook-voting-tps), [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/supreme-court/trump-supreme-court-showdown-major-rulings-loom-rcna349824)

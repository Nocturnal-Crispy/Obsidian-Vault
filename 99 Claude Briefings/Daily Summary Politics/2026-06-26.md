#### 1099 reporting threshold $600 → $2,000 takes effect for 2026 payments
`Heat: Medium` · `Momentum: SUSTAINED` · `Tier 1 — Confirmed` · `Category: Policy`

- **What:** Under OBBBA, 1099-MISC/1099-NEC reporting jumps from $600 to $2,000 for payments made in 2026; 1099-K reverts to $20,000 / 200+ transactions.

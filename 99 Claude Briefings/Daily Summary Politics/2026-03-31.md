## 1. Iran War Day 31: Tanker Struck Off Dubai, Trump Escalates Threats
**Heat: Critical | Momentum: ESCALATED | Source: Tier 1 (CNN, NBC, NPR, Al Jazeera, Bloomberg)**

## 2. DHS Shutdown Day 45: CISA Gutted During Active Cyber War
**Heat: Critical | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 1 (NBC, CBS, Federal News Network, ABC)**

The DHS shutdown — now the longest partial government shutdown in US history — reaches Day 45. The structural deadlock persists: House rejected the Senate's bipartisan bill, passing instead a temporary extension through May 22 with no viable Senate path. Trump signed an executive order directing TSA payments, which began yesterday. However, FEMA, Coast Guard, and CISA employees remain unpaid. The cybersecurity dimension is now critical: CISA has 60% of staff furloughed, 1,000 vacancies, and has been forced into a purely reactive posture during the most intense Iranian cyber campaign against US infrastructure in history. Trump is urging lawmakers to return to Washington and end the shutdown. Republican infighting intensifies as the Iran war spending and DHS funding become tangled in reconciliation negotiations.

## 4. "No Kings" Protest Aftermath — Movement Consolidates
**Heat: High | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 1 (CNN, PBS, NPR, Washington Post)**

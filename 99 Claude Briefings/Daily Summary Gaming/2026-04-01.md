# Gaming Intelligence — Wednesday, April 1, 2026

## 1. April Gaming Calendar — Hades 2 Console, Pragmata, and More
**Heat: High | Momentum: NEW | Source: Tier 1 (Supergiant, Capcom, Xbox Wire, PlayStation Blog)**

April 2026 is shaping up as one of the strongest months of the year for game releases. Hades II launches on PS5 and Xbox Series X|S on April 14 — day one on Game Pass. This is the console debut of Supergiant's roguelike sequel, which has been a massive PC success. Capcom's Pragmata arrives April 17 on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch 2, and PC — a brand-new sci-fi IP featuring protagonist Hugh and android companion Diana on a lunar research station. Other notable April releases include Temtem: Swarm (April 2, 1.0 launch), Darwin's Paradox (April 2), REPLACED (April 14, cyberpunk 2D action-platformer), and Mouse: P.I. for Hire (April 16, rubber hose FPS).

## 2. ARC Raiders Flashpoint — Sustained Early Access Dominance
**Heat: High | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 1 (Embark Studios), Tier 3 (SteamDB)**

ARC Raiders continues its extraordinary early access run following yesterday's Flashpoint update (new ARC, weapons, and content). The game holds an all-time peak of 481,966 concurrent players with sustained daily peaks of 250K–300K. Embark's content cadence is keeping momentum alive. This is one of the strongest early access launches in Steam history, establishing the cooperative extraction shooter as a major genre contender alongside established titles.

## 3. Q1 2026 Steam Player Wrap — Indie Golden Age
**Heat: Medium | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 2/3 (SteamDB, SteamCharts)**

Q1 2026 closes with the indie scene at its strongest ever on Steam:

## 4. Super Meat Boy 3D and Legacy of Kain: Day 2 Assessment
**Heat: Medium | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 1 (Team Meat, Crystal Dynamics)**

Yesterday's dual launch of Super Meat Boy 3D and Legacy of Kain: Ascendance enters its second day. Super Meat Boy 3D (Game Pass day 1) is the franchise's full 3D reimagining with boss encounters and Dark World variants. Legacy of Kain: Ascendance revives the dormant franchise as a 2D action platformer prequel to Soul Reaver. Both are mid-tier releases performing within expectations — neither is breaking into top concurrent territory but both serve enthusiast audiences well.

## 5. Sony Dynamic Pricing — Entrenched Platform Decision
**Heat: High | Momentum: SUSTAINED | Source: Tier 2 (PC Gamer, community)**

Sony's dynamic pricing system continues through the 2026 Spring Sale with no reversal signals. Community backlash remains intense but Sony appears committed. This is now an entrenched platform decision rather than a trial. Neither Microsoft nor Steam have followed suit, suggesting this may remain a Sony-specific strategy. The long-term risk is consumer trust erosion during a period when Game Pass is aggressively competing on value.

# Gaming Intelligence — 2026-06-23

## Valve Officially Announces Steam Machine — $1,049, Launches June 30
**Heat: Critical | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: NEW**

Valve officially announced the Steam Machine today at 10:00 AM PT. The compact 6-inch cube runs SteamOS with a custom AMD Zen 4 CPU and semi-custom RDNA 3 GPU delivering 6x the graphical performance of the Steam Deck. Supports 1440p and 4K gaming, upgradeable DDR5 memory, up to 2TB NVMe SSD.

**Pricing:**
- 512GB (no controller): $1,049
- 2TB (no controller): $1,349
- 2TB + Steam Controller: $1,428

Waitlist open now; preorders open June 25. Launch date: **June 30** (7 days away).

**Why it matters:** Valve is directly competing with console and mini-PC markets simultaneously. The SteamOS ecosystem now spans portable (Deck) and living room (Machine) form factors. This could reshape how PC gaming reaches non-traditional setups.

Sources: [GameSpot](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/steam-machine-price-and-release-date-officially-confirmed-by-valve/), [Engadget](https://www.engadget.com/gaming/pc/valves-steam-machine-launches-in-2026-everything-we-know-so-far-200458597.html), [Geeky Gadgets](https://www.geeky-gadgets.com/valve-steam-machine-june-2026/)

## Star Fox Launches June 25 on Nintendo Switch 2
**Heat: High | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: RISING**

Star Fox arrives exclusively on Switch 2 on June 25 (2 days away). Developed by Velan Studios, it is a reimagining of Star Fox 64 with full voice acting, orchestral soundtrack, and complete visual overhaul. Features classic third-person and new first-person modes. Online 4v4 Battle Mode (Team Star Fox vs Team Star Wolf). Free demo available since June 19.

**Pricing:** $49.99 digital / $59.99 physical.

**Why it matters:** First major Star Fox release in years. Strong early reception of the demo could drive significant Switch 2 hardware sales heading into summer.

Sources: [Nintendo](https://www.nintendo.com/us/store/products/star-fox-switch-2/), [Nintendo Life](https://www.nintendolife.com/news/2026/05/star-fox-is-finally-getting-another-game-out-june-2026-exclusively-on-switch-2)

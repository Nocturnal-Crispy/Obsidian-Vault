# Gaming Intelligence — 2026-06-16

## 1. Xbox Confirms Studio Closures: Ninja Theory Shut Down, Double Fine and Compulsion at Risk
**Heat: Critical | Momentum: NEW | Source: Tier 1 (Confirmed)**

Microsoft has confirmed the closure of Ninja Theory (Hellblade, Senua), just nine days after the studio announced a new game at the Xbox Games Showcase. Double Fine Productions and Compulsion Games (South of Midnight, We Happy Few) are simultaneously in closure or buyout negotiations. This marks the most concentrated single-day contraction in Xbox's first-party history. Studios may have the opportunity to buy themselves back from Microsoft and go independent, though mass layoffs are expected. Compulsion alone could see 100+ workers affected.

The closures fall under the "Xbox Reset" framework outlined by CEO Asha Sharma and chief content officer Matt Booty in a public memo six days prior. The future of Senua (announced for 2027) is now unclear.

**Why it matters:** Microsoft is aggressively cutting studios that don't fit its blockbuster franchise strategy, even ones it just showcased. This signals a fundamental shift in Xbox's first-party philosophy — away from diverse creative bets toward fewer, larger franchise investments. The optics of showcasing a game nine days before shutting down the studio are damaging.

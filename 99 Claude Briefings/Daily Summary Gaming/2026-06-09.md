## 3. SEGA Crazy Taxi: World Tour — AI Controversy Deepening Post-Showcase
**Heat:** Medium | **Momentum:** SUSTAINED | **Source:** Tier 1/2 (Kotaku, PC Gamer, SEGA email marketing)

The Crazy Taxi AI backlash continues into day 2:

- **SEGA marketing push:** "YA YA YA YA YA!" email blast sent to subscribers promoting the reveal
- **Community split:** Initial reveal hype (The Offspring, open-world, original creator) remains strong among casual fans, but the AI disclosure + Denuvo combination continues to generate negative discussion in enthusiast communities
- **Industry watch:** Other studios reportedly monitoring the backlash to calibrate their own AI disclosure strategies

**Strategic Impact:** This story is a test case for the entire industry. If Crazy Taxi sustains its commercial momentum despite the AI controversy, it signals that AI disclosure is a vocal-minority concern. If wishlists stall or decline, it validates the backlash as commercially meaningful. Data over the next 2-4 weeks will be decisive.

# Gaming Intelligence — Saturday, March 21, 2026

## Story 1: Crimson Desert — 2M Sales in 24 Hours, But Pearl Abyss Stock Drops 30%
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** SUSTAINED | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)

Pearl Abyss's Crimson Desert sold over 2 million copies within 24 hours of its March 19 launch, peaking at 239,045 concurrent Steam players and generating massive Twitch viewership. However, mixed reviews (78 Metacritic, initially "Mixed" on Steam now trending toward "Mostly Positive" with 16,000+ reviews) triggered a nearly 30% stock crash for Pearl Abyss on March 19.

The primary community complaint centers on the control scheme complexity. Pearl Abyss has committed to rapid fixes and improvements. Pre-launch analyst estimates pegged ~400,000 copies pre-sold at ~$20M revenue.

**Why it matters:** This is a textbook case of investor expectations diverging from commercial reality. 2M day-one sales is a strong launch by any measure, but the stock market priced in "masterpiece" reviews. The disconnect between sales success and stock performance is a warning signal for publicly traded studios banking on single-title launches.

## Story 2: Slay the Spire 2 — Biggest Steam Launch of 2026
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** RISING | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)

Slay the Spire 2 launched in early access on March 5 and has climbed to a peak of 526,793 concurrent Steam players — the biggest single-game launch on Steam in 2026. The game has entered the Steam all-time top 20 player records, a remarkable achievement for a deck-building roguelike sequel.

**Why it matters:** Slay the Spire 2 validates the "proven indie → massive sequel" pipeline. The early access model continues to produce outsized results when paired with a loyal fanbase and strong word-of-mouth.

## Story 3: Resident Evil Requiem — Highest-Peaking RE on PC
**Heat:** Medium | **Momentum:** NEW | **Source Tier:** 1 (Confirmed)

Capcom's Resident Evil Requiem hit 344,214 concurrent players on Steam during its first weekend, more than doubling the peak of Resident Evil 4 Remake (168,191). This makes it the highest-peaking Resident Evil title on PC.

**Why it matters:** Capcom continues to demonstrate that the RE franchise has strong PC growth potential. Each successive release is setting new concurrency records, suggesting the PC audience for horror-action is still expanding.

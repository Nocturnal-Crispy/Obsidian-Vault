# Politics Intelligence — 2026-06-23

## Congress Passes Landmark Housing Affordability Bill — Heads to Trump's Desk
**Heat: Critical | Tier 1 — Confirmed | Momentum: NEW**

The 21st Century ROAD to Housing Act passed the Senate 85-5 and the House 358-32, making it the largest housing affordability legislation in a generation. The bill now heads to President Trump, who is expected to sign it.

**Key provisions:**
- **"Homes Are For People, Not Corporations"** — bans institutional investors owning 350+ single-family homes from purchasing more
- Eliminates the permanent chassis requirement for manufactured homes, cutting $5,000-$10,000 from construction costs and enabling multi-story/basement designs
- Streamlines building regulations to increase housing supply
- Bipartisan support: co-authored by Elizabeth Warren (D) and Tim Scott (R)

**Why it matters:** This is the most significant federal housing intervention in decades. The corporate investor ban directly targets the post-2020 institutional buyup of single-family housing stock. The manufactured housing reform could meaningfully expand affordable housing options. The overwhelming bipartisan margins (85-5, 358-32) suggest this issue cuts across partisan lines in a way few others do right now.

## ACLU Commits $50M+ to 2026 Midterm Election Monitoring
**Heat: Medium | Tier 2 — Reputable | Momentum: NEW**

The ACLU will spend over $50 million on the 2026 midterms, with half directed toward election administration monitoring. Plans include 100+ paid staff and 3,000+ volunteer leaders focused on voter access, ballot counting, and certification oversight, as Trump seeks expanded control over the election process.

**Why it matters:** This represents one of the largest single-organization election monitoring investments in US history, signaling heightened institutional concern about election integrity heading into the midterms.

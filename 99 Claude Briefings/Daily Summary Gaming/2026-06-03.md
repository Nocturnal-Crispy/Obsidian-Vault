## 2. FF7 Rebirth Launches on Switch 2 — 86 Metascore, "Most Ambitious Port Yet"
**Heat:** High | **Momentum:** NEW | **Source:** Tier 1 (Nintendo Life, RPG Site, Vice, CGMagazine, Square Enix)

Final Fantasy VII Rebirth launched today on Switch 2. Reviews are in:

- **Metascore: 86** (27 reviews) — down from PS5's 92, but a strong showing
- Nintendo Life: 8/10 — "tremendous addition to the growing Switch 2 catalogue"
- Gaming Boulevard: 9.5/10 — "one of Switch 2's defining showcase titles"
- RPGamer: 4.5/5 — "one of the best RPGs currently available on Switch 2"
- **Performance:** Stable frame rates in docked mode; handheld mode shows soft textures, no 60 FPS, longer load times
- **Consensus:** Visual compromises are real but don't meaningfully detract from the experience

**Strategic Impact:** An 86 Metascore for a PS5 → handheld port is an excellent result and validates Switch 2's AAA third-party capability. The "ambitious but imperfect" narrative is the best realistic outcome for Nintendo — it proves the hardware can run demanding current-gen games, even if not at parity. Digital Foundry's technical analysis (expected within 24-48h) will provide the definitive benchmarks. This result should encourage other major publishers to greenlight Switch 2 ports.

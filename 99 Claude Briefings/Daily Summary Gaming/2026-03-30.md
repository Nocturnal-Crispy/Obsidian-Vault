## 2. Sony Dynamic Pricing Expands to 2026 Spring Sale — Backlash Continues
**Heat: High | Momentum: RISING | Source: Tier 2 (PC Gamer, community reports)**

Sony has extended its controversial dynamic pricing system to the 2026 Spring Sale for both PS4 and PS5. The system, which adjusts game prices based on demand signals, continues to generate substantial community backlash. This expansion from the initial US rollout suggests Sony is committed to the model despite negative reception. The pricing system remains one of the most contentious platform decisions in recent memory.

## 4. OmniDrive: Legal Retro Game Ripping Tool Launches
**Heat:** Low | **Momentum:** NEW | **Source:** Tier 2 (Time Extension)

OmniDrive is a new tool that allows users to legally rip optical media from classic consoles (GameCube, Wii, Xbox 360) using a PC Blu-Ray drive. This enables legal preservation of physical game libraries without piracy.

**Strategic Impact:** Niche but significant for the preservation community. Legal ripping tools strengthen the case for emulation as fair use and may influence ongoing preservation policy discussions.

## 4. Supreme Court 8-1: Conversion Therapy Bans Likely Unconstitutional
**Heat: High | Momentum: NEW | Source: Tier 1 (SCOTUSblog, CNN, NPR, NBC, Slate)**

The Supreme Court ruled 8-1 in Chiles v. Colorado that the state's ban on conversion therapy for LGBTQ minors likely violates the First Amendment. Justice Gorsuch wrote for the majority, including liberal justices Kagan and Sotomayor. The ruling does not technically strike down Colorado's law but requires lower courts to apply strict scrutiny — effectively guaranteeing invalidation. More than 20 states have similar bans, all now vulnerable. This is a landmark First Amendment decision with sweeping implications for state regulation of therapeutic speech. Colorado is already moving toward a civil lawsuit framework as an alternative.
